"Keep your flowers fresh"

Trim Stems: Cut 1-2 inches off the stems at a 45-degree angle. Remove Leaves: Take off leaves that will be submerged in water. Use Clean Water: Fill a vase with lukewarm water and add flower food if provided. Change Water: Replace water every 2-3 days and clean the vase. Keep Cool: Place the bouquet away from direct sunlight, heat, and drafts. Avoid Ethylene: Keep flowers away from fruits and vegetables. This should help your flowers last longer and stay vibrant!
